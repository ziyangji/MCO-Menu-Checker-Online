ABOUT THE RPI BookStore

Show the world that you're proud to be a part of the Rensselaer community! At the Collegiate Store, you'll find emblematic RPI clothing and collectibles such as sweatshirts, jackets, t-shirts, polo's and hats from Nike, Under Armour, Champion, League, Vineyard Vines, Columbia, and many others.

The Collegiate Store is located on the first floor of the Student Union at Rensselaer's Troy campus. Shop in-store or online for a wide selection of new, used, and rental textbooks, clothing, gifts, supplies, and computer accessories and products.
